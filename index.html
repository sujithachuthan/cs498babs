BAY AREA BIKE SHARE (BABS)

BABS is a member-ship based system for short term bicycle rentals in the San Francisco Bay Area region. Users can rent bicycles for short journeys between stations though out the city. The system is completely automated for the users with kiosks having fully automated docking facilities. Users can purchase annual memberships (Subscribers) or purchase on Demand (PayPerTrip). The data which I have chosen for this assignment are from rides between September 2015 and August 2016.This is a public dataset (Year 3 Data) and can be downloaded from BABS Site
